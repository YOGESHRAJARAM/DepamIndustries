Our Process

Step:1 Pre-Installation Phase

Project Assessment:

The company will send a representative to assess the site, discuss your needs and preferences, and measure the perimeter. They'll factor in security level requirements, existing structures, and any potential challenges.There are many types of security fencing available, such as chain link, wrought iron, or wood. Consider factors like security level, aesthetics, and budget.Choose the suitable one and get a quote

Step:2 Installation Phase

Fence Panel Installation

Based on the assessment, the company will recommend the most suitable fencing type, post material, and hardware. They'll then order the necessary materials. Our install team will come onsite & wrap up your project in a few days. You just sit back & watch those gorgeous panels go up

Step:3 Completion and Follow-Up

Project Handover and Warranty Information:

Once the fence is complete, the crew chief will do a final inspection to ensure everything meets the agreed-upon standards and safety protocols.The crew will clean up the work area, removing any debris or leftover materials.The company representative will walk you through the installed fence, answer any questions, and provide warranty information for the materials and installation.
